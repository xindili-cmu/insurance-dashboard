Executive Summary

Marketing & Business Development · Lead Pipeline Analysis

Pipeline Overview

The lead pipeline contains 401 total leads generated between June 2024 and February 2025, with a strong overall enrollment rate of 72.8% (292 enrolled). The pipeline peaked in September 2024 at 101 new leads, corresponding to the Fall semester intake season, with a secondary wave in July–August.

Graduate and OPT students convert at the highest rates (75.5% and 75.9%), while Undergraduate leads — the largest segment — trail at 68.6%.

Critical Insight: Group Chat Effect

90.1% vs 53.2%

The single most predictive factor in enrollment is whether a lead is added to a group chat. Leads in a group convert at 90.1%; those not in a group convert at only 53.2% — a 37-point gap.

Key Opportunities

70 no-response leads averaging 5.2 follow-up attempts represent a 17.5% pipeline loss. 188 leads never added to group chat — of which only 53.2% enrolled. Prioritizing group adoption for incoming leads is the highest-ROI tactical change available.

Recommendations

1. Mandate group chat onboarding as the first action after lead creation.

2. Re-engage expired & lost leads (16 total) before the Spring enrollment window closes.

3. Sub-Agent channel shows 100% enrollment on 18 leads — explore scaling this referral model.
